Brightfruit Summer Entrepreneurship Program

Learn essential entrepreneurship skills, work on real team projects, and launch your own business, all in 3 weeks! Find a breakdown of the Summer Program curriculum Below!

Week 1
7/1 - 7/7

Iterative Design Framework

Students work in teams on arts and craft projects as a vehicle to understand the iterative framework of product and business development and the Business Model Canvas tool.

Key Skills: Business Model Canvas, Investigating Value Proposition, Introduction to Customer Interaction, Managing Feedback, Making Meaningful Iterations

Week 2
7/8 - 7/14

Problem Discovery and Customer Segment Immersion

Students split up into teams based on interest in customer segments and conduct problem discovery and solution development for the selected customer segment.

Key Skills: Problem Discovery and Definition, Stakeholder Interaction, Mapping Problem Spaces, Solution Specification, Solution Development and Evaluation

Week 3
7/15 - 7/24

Turn Your Vision into a Business

Students have a chance to bring their visions to life by starting a business venture of their choice.

Key Skills: Visioning, Reflection, Developing Minimum Viable Products, Service Leadership and Managing Team Dynamics

FAQ

Who is this for?

This workshop series is designed with students ages 14-18 in mind. This program is challenging to pushes students out of their comfort zone, so the ideal student would be driven and have an open mind.

How much will this cost?

We are in the process of finalizing the cost per attendee of the workshop. This will depend on the number of attendees. If you are interested in the workshop series, go ahead and sign up on our interest form, and we’ll be in touch with more information and next steps!

Do I have to have a business idea?

Although there will be an opportunity to start your own venture within this workshop series, it is definitely not required that you have a business idea. This course is designed to be an introduction to entrepreneurship that is accessible to those who are curious about entrepreneurship or want to develop entrepreneurial skills without having a serious business idea. However, having some idea of the things you might be excited to try working on would be helpful for making the experience more enjoyable.

Where will this be held?

We are in the process of finalizing the venue for the workshop, which will on the number of attendees, but the location will be in the downtown Ann Arbor area. If this sounds doable to you, go ahead and sign up on our interest form, and we’ll be in touch!
